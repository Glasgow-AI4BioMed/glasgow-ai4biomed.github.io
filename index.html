👋 Welcome to the AI4BioMed Lab

Take a look at what we are working on...

We work on natural language processing, bioinformatics, knowledge graphs, language models, and related methods to extract and infer biomedical knowledge.

The group is led by Dr Jake Lever and Dr Kevin Bryson in the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow.
